Us

One night, at the Wild Colonial Tavern in Providence, Rhode Island, Kathy and Adam, separately, attended a trivia night. Adam was wearing a bright orange Moxie t-shirt and Katherine, offended by his taste in soft-drinks, decided to tease him. Adam did not appreciate being teased by a stranger, cute though she was (is). Kathy's and Adam's companions decided to relocate to the Congress Tavern, and on the way Kathy and Adam had a nice conversation about music. Upon arriving at the Congress Tavern, Adam revealed to Kathy that he was a Cat Guy™, which endeared him to her. She asked him for cat pictures, he obliged. He asked her for her number, she obliged. Now they're getting married.
